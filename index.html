Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AetherAI

Do I need my own API keys to use AetherAI?

Yes, AetherAI requires you to use your own API keys from OpenAI or Google Gemini. This approach ensures your data privacy and gives you full control over your usage and billing.

How do I get API keys for the app?

You can obtain API keys by signing up at the OpenAI website or Google AI Studio. We provide detailed instructions in the app to guide you through the process.

Can I use AetherAI offline?

AetherAI requires an internet connection to communicate with the AI services. However, your conversation history is stored locally on your device for reference.

What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version includes limited monthly messages and image generations with watermarks. The premium version offers unlimited messages, more image generations without watermarks, and access to advanced AI models.

Is my data private when using AetherAI?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Since you use your own API keys, your conversations go directly from your device to the AI provider. We don't store or have access to your conversations or API keys on our servers.

Can I switch between different AI models?

Absolutely! AetherAI allows you to easily switch between different AI providers and models in the settings. This gives you flexibility to use the best model for your specific needs.
